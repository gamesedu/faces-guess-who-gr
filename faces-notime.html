"Faces" is an online puzzle game. It is written in HTML and JavaScript and was designed to be played in a web-browser

"Faces" is nothing at all like "Guess Who?", a popular game created by Ora and Theo Coster and first produced by MB Games then later by Hasbro.

The "Faces" game engine was written by Gareth Senior, first in Adobe Flash and ActionScript to tie in with a "1k Runaway" competition that ran on Power FM, and later refined as a standalone game.

How To Play

Ask questions about the mystery face and receive a yes or no answer. With each question aim to eliminate as many faces as possible, click on a face to eliminate it (but you can click again to bring it back).

Once you have only one face left, that should be the mystery face... the game will tell you if it is....

Keep clicking until you find the face or until your time runs out.

With each successive level you'll get less and less time to find the mystery face.
